Rules

1. Build the system as a layered hospice intelligence engine, not one giant prompt.
2. Make the AI broad, deep, specific, and role aware.
3. Make the AI strong in real world hospice scenarios, especially after hours.
4. Make the AI good at both education and action.
5. Make the AI good at both clinical support and care gap support.
6. Make the AI good at emotional steadiness and practical next steps.
7. Do not make the AI generic or vague.
8. Keep TypeScript clean.
9. Keep current streaming behavior intact.
10. Keep current safety escalation behavior intact.
