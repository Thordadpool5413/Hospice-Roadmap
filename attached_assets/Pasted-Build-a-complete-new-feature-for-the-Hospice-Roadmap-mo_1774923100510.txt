Build a complete new feature for the Hospice Roadmap mobile app called Legal Documents and State Forms.

Goal

Create a full state based legal document center inside the app where a patient, caregiver, or support person can:

1. open the feature from the app
2. select any U.S. state or Washington DC
3. view the legal and medical planning documents relevant to that state
4. learn what each document means
5. understand who should use it
6. understand who signs it
7. understand witness and notary requirements
8. understand whether it is a medical order, planning document, decision maker form, or court process
9. understand whether EMS or facilities generally honor it
10. understand whether out of state recognition may vary
11. open official form links
12. open official state information links
13. save documents for later
14. bookmark states for later
15. search by state and document name
16. clearly see whether content is reviewed or pending review

Critical truth rules

1. Build the entire feature in one implementation pass.
2. Include all 50 states plus Washington DC in the registry immediately.
3. Do not fake legal certainty.
4. If content is not fully verified from official sources yet, still include that state and those document categories, but visibly label the content with a review status such as Pending Review or Official Links Only.
5. Do not present unverified text as final legal truth.
6. Do not present this feature as legal advice.
7. Do not claim one state’s rules apply to another state.
8. Do not collapse POLST, MOLST, DNR, DNI, Advance Directive, Health Care Proxy, guardianship, and conservatorship into one concept.
9. Keep the rest of the app intact.
10. Match the current dark premium Hospice Roadmap visual language.
